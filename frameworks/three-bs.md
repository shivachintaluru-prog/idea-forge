## When to use
The mind generates novel connections most reliably during diffuse-mode activities — Bed (pre-sleep / dream), Bath (shower / bath), Bus (walking / commute / mundane motion). Use this technique for problems that have been chewed on consciously already and need incubation, OR to deliberately escape the convergent attractor of focused thinking.

## The technique
1. Load the problem fully into working memory (10-20 minutes of conscious engagement).
2. Stop. Switch to a low-cognitive-load activity:
   - **Bed**: drift before sleep; capture dawn ideas
   - **Bath**: shower or bath; the warmth + isolation lowers cognitive friction
   - **Bus**: walk, commute, fold laundry, drive a familiar route
3. Do NOT actively try to solve the problem during the activity. Let it stay in peripheral awareness.
4. Carry a capture device. When an idea surfaces, write it down WITHOUT evaluating.
5. Return to focused mode and assess the captured ideas after 30+ minutes.

The technique works because the convergent / executive part of the brain quiets, and the diffuse / associative part (which has access to more connections) gets to drive.

## Anti-mode-collapse prompts
- Do NOT just relabel ideas you already had during focused thinking. Wait for ones that *surprise YOU*.
- The activity must be genuinely low-cognitive-load. Watching a documentary, reading a book, or working out hard counts as focused mode and breaks the technique.
- If you find yourself "trying" during the diffuse period, that's a signal to abandon — start over later.

## Example application (synthetic)
Problem: "Design a feedback system that surfaces dissent without political damage."
After 15 min of focused work, take a shower. During the shower, the idea "What if feedback decayed visibly over time, like an apple?" surfaces. The decay metaphor wasn't generated during focused work — the convergent mode was reaching for "anonymous channels" and "structured templates" instead.

The decay-feedback idea is the kind of output Three Bs is for: a metaphor-driven reframe that opens new territory for refinement.

## Output format
- The diffuse activity used + duration
- 3-5 ideas captured during/right after, with no editing
- Brief note on which idea surprised you most (this is the high-value one)
