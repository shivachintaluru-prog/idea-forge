## When to use
Pose a deliberately absurd statement (the "provocation") about the problem, treat it as if it were true, and follow the consequences. The absurdity creates cognitive distance from convergent territory; the "treat as true" rule forces actual reasoning rather than dismissal.

## The technique
1. Construct 2-3 provocations using the "Po" operators:
   - **Po-Reversal**: assume the OPPOSITE of an obvious truth ("Po: customers don't want better service.")
   - **Po-Exaggeration**: take a parameter to absurd extreme ("Po: support response time is 30 days.")
   - **Po-Elimination**: remove a "necessary" component ("Po: the product has no UI.")
   - **Po-Hybrid**: combine with completely unrelated domain ("Po: this is operated like a monastery.")
   - **Po-Fantasy**: state something physically impossible ("Po: the customer experiences the product before signing up.")
2. For each provocation, take it AS IF TRUE. What follows? What would a real implementation look like that satisfies the provocation? Generate 2-3 ideas per provocation.
3. The surviving ideas — those that, after extracting from the provocation, would actually be valuable — are the harvest.

## Anti-mode-collapse prompts
- Do NOT immediately interpret the provocation back to a sensible version. The discomfort of holding the absurdity is where the work happens.
- Generate ideas that LITERALLY satisfy the provocation, even if they seem unworkable. The unworkable form often points to a workable core.
- The Fantasy operator is the most powerful but most resisted. Force at least one fantasy provocation per session.

## Example application (synthetic)
Problem: "Improve user retention for a productivity app."

Provocations + consequent ideas:
- **Po: users should feel WORSE after using the app.** Idea: post-session reflection prompt that confronts users with their unmet goals — discomfort drives meaningful re-engagement, not dopamine.
- **Po: the app is operated like a monastery.** Idea: deliberate periods of silence in the app — no notifications, no metrics surfaced — followed by structured reflection. Retention via depth, not frequency.
- **Po: there is no app, only ritual.** Idea: the "app" is a daily 5-minute physical practice (handwriting on paper) and the digital component is a private, after-the-fact log.

The third idea would never have surfaced from "improve retention" framed conventionally.

## Output format
- 2-3 provocations
- 2-3 ideas per provocation
- 3-5 surviving harvest ideas after extracting valuable cores
